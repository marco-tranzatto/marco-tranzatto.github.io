Marco Tranzatto

Hi, my name is Marco and I am working as Robotics Engineer at the Robotic Systems Lab (RSL) of ETH Zürich. I am the lead engineer for the quadruped robot ANYmal of the CERBERUS Team, which is currently competing in the Darpa SubT Challenge.

Before joining RSL, I worked for one year for the Autonomous Systems Lab (ASL) - ETH Zurich, where I was part of the MBZIRC 2017 Team which scored 2nd place in the Grand Challenge.
I also spent six months as Robtics Engineer in Aerotain, where I developed a ROS-based simulation for Skye, an indoor blimp. Before that, I worked with a 10 person team developing an unmanned airship with acrobatic capabilities during the summer Zeppaloon project with Kongsberg Maritime - Norway.

Once in a while, I post pictures about ANYmal and in general about my work on my Twitter account.
